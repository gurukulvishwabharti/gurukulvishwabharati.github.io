Message from the desk of vice chancellor……....

Because we can

It is my privilege and pleasure to welcome you, the budding managers to Jamia Hamdard, a centre of innovation and excellence where we nurture young talents. Our major emphasis of imparting education is to encourage curiosity and innovativeness amongst our students and lay a foundation from where they can acquire quick learning ability and adaptability. The student-centric approach brings about a three-sixty degree development of students that empowers them to face the challenges in this highly competitive and ever changing world. A challenging academic program, and individual attention for our students combined with many extra-curricular opportunities are all part of Jamia Hamdard experience.

Our Mission......jamia hamdard is an institute of higher education deemed to be University located in New Delhi, india. it is a goverment-funded university.established in 1989,was formally inaugurated by the Prime Minister of India shri Rajiv Gandhi on august 1,1989. it was awarded the institute of eminence by ministry of Human Resource Developement.Our aim is to provide education to all.We are committed to produce quality man-power equipped with human and social values to integrate excellent technical skill, social character,leadership,creativity and innovation for the benefit of mankind and sustainable development of the nation. We creating future to learn more!
